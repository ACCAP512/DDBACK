# legal/ — attorney-ready templates (drafts)

⚠️ **These are DRAFT TEMPLATES, not legal advice and not finalized agreements.** They are starting points
that encode the compliance requirements analyzed in [`../docs/COMPLIANCE.md`](../docs/COMPLIANCE.md). A
licensed **customs attorney** (for the customs-business / EULA structure) and a **privacy attorney** (for
the DPA / privacy policy) must review, complete the bracketed terms, and finalize them before use or any
real client data.

| File | Purpose | Key compliance hooks |
|---|---|---|
| `EULA-TEMPLATE.md` | Software license / EULA | Field-of-use (licensed filer / self-file own account); decision-support & no-advice disclaimer; flat-fee only; estimate-not-guarantee; AS-IS + limitation of liability |
| `DPA-TEMPLATE.md` | Data processing / service-provider addendum | CCPA service-provider terms; EEI confidentiality (15 CFR 30.60); local/non-retention; 19 CFR 111.24 flow-down; GDPR Art. 28 (if triggered) |
| `PRIVACY-POLICY-TEMPLATE.md` | Public privacy policy | No-sale / no-retention of trade data; local processing; CCPA/GDPR rights |

For the **asset-sale** documents (IP assignment + representations & warranties), see the recommended reps
in [`../AI-DISCLOSURE.md`](../AI-DISCLOSURE.md) and `docs/COMPLIANCE.md §3` — those are papered by the
buyer's/seller's transactional counsel at the deal.
